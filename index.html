About

Let me introduce myself.

Hi there! My name is Princes Mae Palma, but you can call me “Cess” for short. I am an 18-year-old living in the beautiful city of Makati, which happens to be my favorite place in the world. I have two brothers and we are a close-knit family. In fact, my family is my greatest strength in life - without them, I wouldn’t be who I am today. I’m a strong and determined woman who isn’t afraid to take risks and try new things, even if they may seem dangerous. Life is an adventure, and I am always ready for the next challenge. I am currently studying at Benigno S. Ninoy Aquino High School, where I am majoring in TVL Cookery. I am grateful to be studying at Benigno because the school provides an excellent education and the teachers are both knowledgeable and kind. In addition to gaining valuable knowledge and skills, I have also had the opportunity to meet many interesting people and make new friends.

Journey

My Senior Highschool Journey

My senior high school journey was a lot of fun. I met kind teachers, supportive classmates, and an amazing adviser who never left our side. My favorite teacher was our adviser, Ma’am Resus. Being part of the Rosewood section was a great experience. Even when we faced challenges with school work, we remained united. I made friends who treated me like family and were always there to help and support me. If I hadn’t been in the Rosewood section, I might never have met them. One of my favorite experiences was our on-the-job training (OJT). I met so many kind people who treated me like a sister and taught me new things. They guided me and supported me in pursuing my dreams. I also learned many new skills, like cooking different foods and making desserts. It was challenging at first, but it became fun once I got the hang of it. Overall, my senior high school journey was a happy one. Despite the struggles with assignments and activities, I learned so much. I am grateful for the experience and hope to have similar ones in the future, even if we won’t all be in the same class anymore.
